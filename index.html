What to see.

Three districts, three eras

Every Prague quarter belongs to a different century — from royal pathways to industrial revival and rebellious art.

№01

Staré Město

Old Town

A labyrinth of cobbled lanes, the Gothic spires of Týn Church and the Astronomical Clock striking every hour since 1410.

№02

Malá Strana

Lesser Quarter

Red-tiled roofs nestled beneath the Castle walls. Baroque gardens, hushed courtyards and the cleanest morning air in the city.

№03

Žižkov

The Local Rebel

Brutalist TV tower crawled by David Černý's giant babies. Defiant, working-class, with the highest pub density in Europe.
